Top 5 Types of Cats

This is webpage dedicated to showing what types of cats I think are the best. While all cats are perfect and beautiful, there are a select few that I think shine above the rest.

Keep scrolling

5. Maine Coon

Our number five spot goes to the Maine Coon! These fluffy guys are so big and soft-looking. People say they don't make good lap cats, and all I have to say to that is, not with that attitude, you haters. The biggest issue with these guys is that they are pretty expensive and require a lot of care, so they may not be a good choice for poor college students.

4. Calico

For number four, we have the Calico! These cuties are just so nice to look at--look at their fur and their little faces. One interesting about calicos is that most of them are female cats, and it's very rare to see a male calico cat. They also represent good luck in Japan!

3. Black Cats

I don't know if this a specific breed, but black cats rule. They are so pretty to look at, but they get a bad rep becuase they're seen as symbols of bad luck. This is wrong--they are just little guys.

2. Orange Cats

Once again, this is color and not a breed. These guys are awesome though, they're so silly and so stupid. My sister has only ever had orange cats, so I may be biased, but they are so affecionate and I love them.

1. Grey Cats

Number one spot goes to grey cats! Another name for them is Russian Blue cats, but I like calling them grey because it's easier. These guys get the number one spot because they're cute and soft and awesome. Some may say I'm biased because my cat, Beatrice, is a grey cat, but this is not true.
